FAQ

How often can I get a free copy of my credit reports?

You can obtain a free copy of your credit report every year from any of the three credit bureaus at AnnualCreditReport.com. In certain circumstances you may be able to obtain additional free reports.

Why should I check my free credit report?

40% of credit reports contain inaccurate information. Your credit report can be used by a number of businesses and government agencies to determine everything from credit rates to employment offers and government benefits. Incorrect information in your credit report can have a devastating effect on your ability to get or keep a job. Make sure the information in your report is correct so you are not unfairly penalized.
